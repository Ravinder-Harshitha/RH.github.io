Mountain Pose

Sanskrit name: Tadasana



How to do

Stand still, with your chest open and broad and your hands at your sides, and feel your feet on the floor and the sensations in your legs and back.

Benifits of this pose

This pose will show if you have any imbalances in your shoulders and give you clues about what you need to work on.
