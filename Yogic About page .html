About Us

Counteract the stresses of modern life by becoming more mindful and compassionate.

Our Vision

Our vision is to offer a towards a healthy lifestyle accessible to people from all walks of life and people who are willing to transform their life through yoga. We at the yogic community believe that practicing yoga, meditation and healthy lifestyle regularly greatly enhances a person’s physical, mental and spiritual health.

We aim to create a world where all can access the tools of yoga and community for personal and communal transformation. Yogic Yoga community is a place where people come together and support one another in their goals toward a healthier lifestyle.

Our Mission

We offer services that promote a mind-body-spirit through yoga and meditation classes with mats and props supplied at no charge, workshops & trainings. We offer space and guidance for individuals towards fullfillment of life through yoga and meditation techniques.

We are commited to our purpose of providing opportunities and means to uplift themselves and others on their path to wellness. We strive to continue to offer an inclusive and flexible schedule to be able to serve a diverse population so that everyone has access to the benefits of a yoga practice.

Events and Workshops

Yoga events and workshops are a great way to bring the yoga community together and offer a unique experience that goes beyond a typical yoga class.

These events range from outdoor yoga and retreats, to workshops that partner with local animal shelters or cat cafes to promote adoption while practicing yoga surrounded by furry friends 1.

These events help forge community partnerships, give back to important causes, and drive more diverse clientele towards yoga.
